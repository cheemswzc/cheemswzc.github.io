Host susceptibility

The coliform bacterium disease of animal can happen in a variety of livestock, poultry, breed economy

Onset seasons

The seasonal characteristics of escherichia coli infection among animal groups are not very obvious.
It can occur all year round in pigs, but as long as it occurs between calving stage and weaning stage,
it is associated with the susceptible age of pigs. Calves and lambs are most common in winter and spring.
Escherichia coli infections in other animals occur all year round with no obvious seasonality.
