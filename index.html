Ethanol

Ethanol (also called ethyl alcohol, grain alcohol, drinking alcohol, or simply alcohol) is an organic chemical compound.
It is a simple alcohol with the chemical formula C2H6O.








Introduce

· General primary alcohol

· Ethanol is often used as a disinfectant and antiseptic

· Industrial ethanol often mixed with toxic substances to prevent drinking.

· Ethanol vapour can be mixed with air to form an explosive mixture.
