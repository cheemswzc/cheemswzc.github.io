Ethanol has the chemical properties of general primary alcohol and is more active. It is an important raw material and solvent for organic synthesis.Ethanol is often used in the pharmaceutical industry as a disinfectant and antiseptic. Ethanol is also widely used as a drink and fuel. In the past, industrial ethanol was often mixed with small amounts of toxic substances such as methanol to prevent drinking.
